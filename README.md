# Gatekeeper: A Decentralized Content Monetization Platform

Gatekeeper is a premium content platform designed to empower creators by enabling them to monetize their work directly through decentralized technology. Whether you are a writer, videographer, or educator, Gatekeeper provides a gateway to get paid for the content you create.

## Features

- **Multi-Format Support:** Creators can publish various types of content, including text articles, videos, podcasts, and full courses.
- **Direct Monetization:** Set your own price for your work in USDC.
- **On-Chain Registration:** All content is registered on the Solana blockchain to ensure transparency and secure transactions.
- **Secure Payments:** Users pay directly in USDC to unlock specific articles or media, ensuring creators are compensated for every access.

## How It Works

### For Creators

1. **Connect Your Wallet:** Start by connecting a compatible wallet (such as MetaMask with Solana support).
2. **Create Content:** Use the "Start Publishing" interface to enter your content details:
   - Provide a Title (e.g., "Understanding Solana").
   - Write a Description or the main body of the content.
   - Set a Price in USDC for access.
3. **Publish:** Sign the transaction through your wallet to register the article on-chain.

### For Consumers

1. **Browse Content:** Users can browse the library of existing articles and media.
2. **Unlock Access:** To read or view premium content, users pay the required USDC amount directly from their account.
3. **Instant Access:** Once the transaction is confirmed, the content is unlocked for viewing.
